Ngoni's Spaghetti Bolognese

This Vietnamese spaghetti bolognese is a delicious Asian twist on an Italian classic.
Inspired By:Marion's Kitchen

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Garlic
  • Mushroooms
  • Spaghetti
  • Spring Onions

Recipe Instructions

  1. Finely grate the parmesan cheese, chop the mushroom slices, also peel and crush the garlic cloves.
  2. Infuse the olive oil with garlic by heating some cloves in an oil-coated pan until its fragrant.
  3. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
  4. Add butter, mushrooms, and chilli flakes into another pan - fry and stir until the butter is melted.
  5. Add soy sauce to the pan, simmer for a minute then turn off the heat.
  6. When the pasta is done, drain the water.
  7. Turn on the pan heat once again, add the pasta in and stir until it has a thick, glossy coating.
  8. Turn off the heat and top with drizzle of garlic oil, parmesan cheese, and finely chopped spring onions.
  9. Serve!
